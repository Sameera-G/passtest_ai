What is IELTS?

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is one of the most recognized English proficiency exams in the world. It evaluates the English language skills of non-native speakers who wish to study, work, or migrate to English-speaking countries.

Reading Section

40 questions that test comprehension, detail, inference, and opinion recognition. You‚Äôll read passages from books, journals, and newspapers.

Listening Section

Four audio recordings of native English speakers followed by questions. This section tests your ability to understand facts, opinions, and follow ideas logically.

Writing Section

Two writing tasks that include describing visual data and responding to a specific point of view or problem. It evaluates organization, vocabulary, and grammar.

Speaking Section

A face-to-face interview with an examiner. It covers introduction, short speech, and discussion topics. It tests fluency, vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation.
